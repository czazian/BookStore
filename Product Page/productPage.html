Description:

A 17-year-old girl named Suzume lives with her aunt in the remote port city of Kyushu. One day on his way to school, he ran into a handsome young man who said, "I'm looking for the door"....


Suzume followed the young man named Souta to an abandoned location in the forest. But what was there was just an old white door in the middle of the ruins. As if summoned, Suzume turned the doorknob. But suddenly strange things started to happen...


What happened to Suzume and Souta after that? Follow Suzume's journey to 'lock' the door that connects the present, past and future!
